# RFC-010 — Refine Graph Layout Readability

## Objective

Refine the horizontal reasoning DAG without changing reasoning behavior,
replay, progressive reveal, session format, or backend contracts.

## Vertical Distribution

The X coordinate remains the fixed reasoning timeline. Primary node Y positions
use deterministic preferred anchors instead of one shared center line:

```text
Question     center
Hypothesis   center - 70px
Evidence     center - 20px
Perspective  center + 40px
Conflict     center + 80px
Judge        center + 10px
Conclusion   center
```

The renderer may scale these offsets to the available canvas and add a small,
deterministic variation. It must not use random or free force-directed
placement. Any collision resolution may move nodes on Y only and must preserve
their stage column.

## Satellites

Evidence and Perspective satellites use a larger radial cluster around their
parent (roughly 30–50% more spacing than RFC-009). They remain data-driven and
do not alter node semantics.

## Constraints

- Do not change backend, API, reasoning order, session lifecycle, replay,
  reveal timing, or export formats.
- Keep left-to-right stage/round ordering and smooth forward edges.
- The resulting graph must be structured and readable, not a flat timeline or
  an unconstrained force graph.
