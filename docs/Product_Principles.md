# 00 - Product Principles

# Thesision

> Reason Before Decision.

---

## Overview

Thesision is an AI-native reasoning platform designed to help software engineers analyze complex technical questions through transparent, evidence-driven reasoning rather than direct answer generation.

Instead of acting like a traditional chatbot, Thesision orchestrates multiple AI reasoning stages including hypothesis generation, evidence evaluation, multi-perspective analysis, and final synthesis before producing a conclusion.

The goal is not simply to answer questions, but to expose how conclusions are formed.

---

# Core Philosophy

AI should not immediately generate answers.

AI should build trustworthy reasoning before reaching conclusions.

Transparency is more valuable than speed when solving complex engineering problems.

---

# Product Principles

## 1. Reasoning First

Every conclusion must originate from a structured reasoning process.

No direct answer generation.

---

## 2. Evidence Over Opinion

Claims should always be supported by evidence whenever possible.

If evidence is insufficient, the system must explicitly communicate uncertainty.

---

## 3. Transparency

Users should be able to inspect every important reasoning step.

Reasoning is not hidden.

---

## 4. Multi-Perspective Thinking

Complex engineering problems rarely have only one correct answer.

Different perspectives should be explored before reaching conclusions.

---

## 5. Human-Centered AI

AI assists engineers.

AI does not replace engineering judgment.

Final decisions always belong to the user.

---

## 6. Iterative Reasoning

Reasoning may continue through multiple iterations until:

- sufficient confidence is achieved,
- major conflicts are resolved,
- or maximum reasoning budget is reached.

---

## 7. Engineering Focus

The first version of Thesision focuses exclusively on Software Engineering and AI Engineering.

Expansion into other domains belongs to future releases.

---

## 8. AI is the Product

AI is not an additional feature.

Without AI reasoning, Thesision cannot function.

---

## 9. Explainability

Every important conclusion should be explainable.

Users should understand not only what the answer is, but why the system reached it.

---

## 10. Future-Proof Architecture

The architecture should remain modular.

Reasoning agents, evidence providers, and orchestration components should be replaceable without redesigning the entire system.

---

# MVP Principles

The MVP prioritizes:

- simplicity
- reliability
- transparency
- engineering usefulness

The MVP does NOT attempt to solve every reasoning problem.

---

# Non Goals

The MVP is not:

- a chatbot
- a search engine
- an autonomous coding assistant
- a replacement for software engineers
- a scientific paper generator

---

# Long-Term Vision

Enable AI systems that reason visibly, transparently, and collaboratively before making decisions.
